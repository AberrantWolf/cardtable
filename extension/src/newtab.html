SSO deep-link restore. Reopening a card that lives behind single sign-on often dumps you on the app's home page instead of the page you saved. With this on, cardtable remembers the page you meant to open and — once it sees the tab bounce through a known login host and return to the same site — finishes the trip for you. It only ever redirects within that one site, only right after a login bounce, and gives up after 90 seconds. If you don't use SSO apps, leave it off.
